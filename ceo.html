About the Founder

Md Ehtesham, the visionary Founder & CEO, popularly known as "Md Shums," is a passionate young entrepreneur, technology enthusiast, and AI learner dedicated to building innovative digital solutions for the future. He completed his Middle School education from Beacon English School and pursued his High School education in the Science stream from St Sebastian's School, successfully completing his school journey in 2023.

Currently, he is pursuing a Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) from Amity University, one of India's leading private universities. Along with academics, he has developed strong skills in Web Development, Artificial Intelligence, Database Management, UI/UX Design, Freelancing, and Digital Innovation. His mission is to combine creativity with technology to create impactful solutions that simplify lives and empower businesses.

With a strong vision, leadership mindset, and continuous learning attitude, Md Shums believes that age is never a barrier to success. His dedication toward technology and innovation reflects his dream of inspiring young minds to become creators rather than just consumers of technology.
